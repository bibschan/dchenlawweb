About David Chen

David is an avid traveller. He travels annually to Asia and he has been to most provinces in China. He has also travelled to other Asian countries including Japan, South Korea, Singapore, and Malaysia. At home, he has also travelled extensively in Canada. Proudly, he has been to all provinces and territories of Canada except Nunavut.

Experience with Law

Education

David obtained his Juris Doctor (J.D.) degree from the University of Ottawa with the honor of Cum Laude. Prior to the commencement of his legal career, he worked at the Department of Health of the Federal Government of Canada for four years, where he developed expertise and accumulated knowledge of the food and drug laws in Canada. For his science undergraduate degree from the University of British Columbia, he specialized in Cell Biology and Genetics with a Minor in Commerce.
