about our firm

Since our inception, D.CHEN LAW has been about the legal services, not the frills around it: we intend to let it stay that way.

We are located in the heart of downtown New Westminster, but with pride we provide our services and meet with our clients in Richmond, Burnaby, Vancouver, and other cities in the Lower Mainland Area with our mobile office.

D.Chen Law's goal is to provide tailored legal services to our clients, deliver them at the most timely moment, and at the place where D.CHEN Law is most needed.

Our approach

We provide a full range of legal services that goes from incorporating your first business to litigating on your behalf to collect that long-overdue debt.

At D.CHEN LAW, we believe in keeping our practice diverse so that we can provide comprehensive care for our clients from their day-to-day needs to their complex business purchases.

Mission statement

We deliver exceptional legal services to commerce, business, immigrations and property sectors combining legal tradition and modern technology.
